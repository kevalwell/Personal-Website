Capriza POC

Capriza is an online 3rd party platform that allows for rapid development of mobile ‘Zapps’ backed by web based applications. The major benefit is the rapid deployment of previously non mobile-friendly web based applications as a mobile Zapp. While developing a POC led by John Howarth, there were many interactions and demonstrations form the Capriza Team. The software is rather intuitive to the user, but close contact with Michael Kowalski of Capriza was necessary to flush out use cases, and ultimately the POC.

Benefits

Rapid Mobile Deployment

Work out of the Office

Turn any web based application into a mobile friendly Zapp, for use out of the office.

Displaying Benefits with Ease

Asphalt Value Calculator presents information in a uniform, and easy to read fashion. Users can easily view their value proposition and take advantage of it with order functionality.

Low Cost Development

Development costs are low considering how intuitive this software is. Users may create an app within 8 hours of being exposed to Capriza.

Intuitive UX

Capriza allows for compression of large web based applications. Block out the noise of unnecessary information, to allow the user to focus on the most important functionality.
