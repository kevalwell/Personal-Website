Office 365 & OneDrive Adoption

Project goal is to drive enterprise adoption of Microsoft Office 365 Suite, and OneDrive. My team and I developed a short compilation video of O365 commentary. Also, piloting in Morristown I worked with Valerie Palermo, Rob Jukic, Nick Mitricka, Sharon Young, Kunal Patel, and Serena Badro to create ‘Hackathon’ style events where interested parties may come and enjoy pizza, and learn about an interesting and relevant topic (O365 Adoption as of 08/03).

Enterprise Adoption

Deriving Value

Adoption solutions will provide greater corporate utilization and integration. The Hackathons & Awareness video helps maximize ROI for the enterprise on the Microsoft Office suite. Microsoft Online allows for collaboration on OneNote, OneDrive, and the entire Office 365 suite.

OneDrive users may use the service to store an unlimited amount of their data on secure online servers. OneDrive provides reliable storage with no additional hardware to manage.

Working across devices from anywhere

Online & Sync functionality allow for users to work from any device, anywhere, anytime.
